do you like risotto?

The main ingredient in a Risotto is shallot sautéed in butter, dry white wine, hot stock, vegetables or mushroom.
    Reasons why you should try Risotto!
    1.Firstly, what makes risotto special is its luxurious, creamy texture. Unlike other rice dishes where the grains are separate, risotto is made by slowly cooking arborio or carnaroli rice in broth, stirring constantly, which releases starch and creates a rich and velvety consistency.
    2.It's simple, versatile, cheap and above all, it is incredibly tasty!
3.Risotto is believed to have originated around Lombardy. The first recipe identifiable as risotto dates from 1809. It includes rice sautéed in butter, sausages, bone marrow, and onions with broth with saffron gradually added
